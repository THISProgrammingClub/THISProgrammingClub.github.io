How to Send Announcements

  1. press to enter the announcement page

    • By default, the app automatically subscribes the student council, but you can un-subscribe it manually

  2. press to select clubs

    • please do not subscribe the clubs that you are not in

  3. you can scroll to / search clubs and subscribe by selecting its checkbox

  4. you can also un-subscribe clubs by de-selecting its corresponding checkbox

  5. after selecting all the clubs to subscribe, press again to return to announcement page to see your announcements
