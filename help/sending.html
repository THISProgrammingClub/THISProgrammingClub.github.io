How to Send Announcements

(This feature is only available to registered clubs and school organizations)

  1. Contact with the THIS Programming Club members

    • You will receive a name and password to log in inside the app
    • Please do not tell anyone else the information. If you accidentally did so, contact us to set a new password

  2. press to enter the posting page

  3. log in with your given information (see step one) and post announcements for your club!
